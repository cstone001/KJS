About Kevin J. Stone

Kevin was born in Danville, Virginia, and currently resides in Durham, North Carolina. He attended Durham Technical Community College, where he received an Associate of Arts degree in Psychology. He then went to Devry University, where he received a Bachelor of Science degree in Technical Management. Kevin is a 2x award-winning actor, receivng the 2013 Down East Award for Best Actor and the Director's Choice Award for Best Actor for the short film, "Work of Art - The Power Within". He has been nominated for 7 awards for Best Actor thus far.


Kevin knew at a very young age that he wanted to become an actor. Growing up, he would always watch televison shows and movies, and imitate the scenes that he was watching, but making it his own, by putting his own spin on it, trying to act better than the actors in the scenes. Kevin knows that this is what he was born to do. He takes every role he gets very seriously, and studies the character, answering the 5 W's (Who, What, Where, When, Why) about the character.


Kevin is currently gearing up to film filming season 3 of the hit web series, titled, "Lovers Lane". In addition to that he will also be starring in a feature film, titled, "Check Please: 30 Dates in 30 Days".
